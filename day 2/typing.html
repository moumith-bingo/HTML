Microsoft Patents Ones and Zeros

Unexpected development shakes up computer industry

Redmond,WA: In what Bill Gates an unfortunate but neccessary step to
protect our intelectual property from theft and exploitation by competitors, the Microsoft
Corporation patented the numbers one and zero Monday

With the patent, Microsoft's rivals are prohibited from manufacturing or selling products
containing zeros and ones unless a royalty fee of 10 cents per digit used is paid to the software giant
