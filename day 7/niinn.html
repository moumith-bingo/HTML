Fox

Fox is a common name for many species
of carnivorous mammals belonging to the
Canidae Family. Foxes are small to medium-
sized canids(slighly smaller than the medium-
sized domestic dogs), characterized by
possesing a long narrow snout, and a bushy tail(or brush)

Memberss of about 37 species are referred
to as foxes, of which only 12 species.
