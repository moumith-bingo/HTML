Welcome to BlueJscript

This help will tell you what BlueJscript is not and where to go for further help

BlueJscript is not

A learning environment: Do not expect BlueJscript to teach you the syntax of javascript.To find out
about javascript you will need to look at references in the BlueJscript site

A complete browser system:Most of the BlueJscript exapmles have been written in standard HTML.
But they they contain link to other sites that use the most advanced HTML and they are not compatible
with the standard viewer that is embedded here. If you want to browse the internet use you preferred browser

A tutorial on webservices:You will realize that you can drive BlueJscript through webservices. There
will be examples on how to do it but you will need to do your homework on understanding the technology
